Free vs. Premium - What's the Difference?

3 min read · Jul 2026

Every skill on X Freeze falls into one of two tiers, built for two different jobs.

Free / Instant Skills - for everyday tasks

These are quick, copy-paste tools for the things you need done right now: draft a meeting agenda, write exit-interview questions, summarize an email thread, build a SWOT analysis. No setup, no learning curve - copy the skill, paste it into Grok, Claude, Cursor, or ChatGPT, and you have a usable draft in seconds.

Built for: individuals, students, freelancers, anyone who wants a fast, reliable starting point instead of a blank page.

What to expect: a clear, focused workflow (usually 3-5 steps) that gets you 80% of the way there quickly. Simple by design - not because it's unfinished, but because the task itself doesn't need more.

Premium Skills - for teams and businesses

These are the deep, consultant-grade versions: a founder's weekly decision memo, a security threat-model review, a real-estate maintenance risk assessment, a churn-risk playbook for customer success teams. Each one is written with real professional judgment - the tradeoffs, the pitfalls, the exact document a specialist in that field would actually produce.

Built for: teams and businesses making decisions with real stakes, where "good enough" isn't good enough - legal, financial, hiring, security, and strategic work that deserves more than a generic template.

What to expect: genuinely distinct, situation-specific guidance - not the same recipe with the topic swapped. Every premium skill in a sensitive category (legal, HR, health, security, finance) also comes with the right professional-review flags built in, so you know exactly when to loop in a real expert.

Why we split it this way

Free skills are meant to be fast and broad - one for almost anything you'd want to draft in five minutes. Premium skills are meant to be narrow and deep - built for the moments where getting it right actually matters.

No X Freeze paywall on copy. Premium is a depth label, not a checkout gate. You copy free and premium skill prompts the same way on the Skills page. (Grok Imagine templates are different - running those needs Super Grok / Super Grok Heavy on Grok’s side.)

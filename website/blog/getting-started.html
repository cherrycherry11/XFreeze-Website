How to Use X Freeze

2 min read · Updated Jul 2026

X Freeze has three things: templates, motion prompts, and AI skills. Templates help you create visuals in Grok. Motion prompts turn stills into video. Skills help you finish work faster in Grok, Cursor, Claude, or similar AI tools.

Want the full deep dive (free vs premium, Super Grok, connectors, troubleshooting)?

Use templates

  1. Open the Templates page.
  2. Pick a category like Product, Mockups, or Style Edit.
  3. Click a template. It opens in Grok.
  4. Add your image or product and generate.

Use motion prompts

  1. Open the Prompts page.
  2. Pick a motion category (camera, product spin, lighting, and more).
  3. Copy a prompt.
  4. Paste it into Grok Imagine (or another image-to-video tool) with your image.

Use skills

  1. Open the Skills page.
  2. Find one skill for what you need.
  3. Click Copy prompt.
  4. Paste it into your AI tool.
  5. Run the slash command, like /ad-copy or /blog-outline.

Best first try

  1. Use one Product or Style Edit template.
  2. Generate three versions.
  3. Copy a motion prompt and turn your favorite still into a short video.
  4. Copy one skill like /ad-copy, /instagram-caption, or /seo-meta.
  5. Use the skill to write the caption, ad text, or listing text.

Privacy

Quick note on data. I keep it simple - this is a small site, not a surveillance company.

This is the privacy page for X Freeze (xfreeze.com). Updated July 2026. For usage rules (including no resale), see Terms.

What I may collect

  • If you message me - name, email, and what you write in the contact form
  • If you sign in - account info handled by Supabase (email / social login)
  • If you tip or buy something - payment sites (Buy Me a Coffee, Stripe, PayPal, etc.) handle cards. I only see basic order/confirm stuff, not your full card number
  • Normal website stuff - browser, device, pages visited, and prefs stored on your device (like light/dark mode)

What I don’t do

  • I don’t sell your personal info
  • I don’t get your Gmail, Drive, Notion, etc. when you use a skill - those stay in your tools
  • When you open Grok or another site, their privacy rules apply over there, not here

Why I use it

To run the site, reply to you, fix bugs, stop abuse, and (if you buy something) complete the order. That’s pretty much it.

Cookies & storage

I use basic cookies / local storage for things like theme preference and staying logged in. You can clear them in your browser anytime.

Questions or delete my data

Just ask - contact@xfreeze.com or the contact form. I’ll help.

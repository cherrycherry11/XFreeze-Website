Legal

Privacy

What data we use, why we use it, and the choices you have. We keep it to what the site actually needs.

This is the Privacy Policy for X Freeze (xfreeze.com). It explains what information we collect when you use the site and what we do with it. For the rules on using and buying our content, see our Terms of Service and Refund Policy. Last updated July 2026.

Information we collect

  • When you contact us: your name, email address, and the message you send through the contact form or by email.
  • When you create an account or sign in: account details such as your email address and, if you use Google or X to sign in, the basic profile information those services share. Accounts and sign-in are handled for us by Supabase.
  • When you buy or subscribe: your purchase is processed by our payment provider when checkout is available. Card and billing details are handled by that provider; we receive order confirmation details only and do not store full card numbers.
  • When you send a tip: tips go through Buy Me a Coffee, which handles that payment under its own privacy terms.
  • Usage and device information: standard details such as your browser type, device, and the pages you view, plus preferences saved on your device, for example your light or dark theme and whether you are signed in.

How we use your information

We use the information above to run and improve the site, to reply to your messages, to create and manage your account, to complete and support your purchases and subscriptions, to keep records of orders, and to protect the site against fraud and abuse. We may also use it to send you service messages about your account or a purchase.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with the service providers that help us run X Freeze, and only so they can do their job. These include Supabase for accounts and sign-in, a payment provider (when checkout is live), Buy Me a Coffee for tips, and our hosting provider. Each of them handles your data under its own privacy terms. We may also share information if the law requires it or to protect our rights and users.

Things we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not read or collect the contents of your connected tools, such as Gmail, Drive, or Notion, when you use a skill. Those stay inside your own accounts.
  • When you open Grok or another third-party site, its privacy rules apply there, not ours.

Cookies and local storage

We use a small amount of cookies and browser local storage for things like keeping you signed in and remembering your theme choice. These are needed for the site to work properly. You can clear them in your browser at any time, though some features may stop working if you do.

How long we keep it

We keep account and order information for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period after that, so we can support purchases, meet record-keeping needs, and handle disputes. Contact messages are kept for as long as we need them to help you.

Your choices and rights

You can ask us to show you the information we hold about you, to correct it, or to delete it. You can also close your account. To make a request, email us and we will help. Some information may need to be kept where the law requires it, for example basic records of a completed payment.

International users

X Freeze is available worldwide. If you use the site, you understand that your information may be processed by us and our service providers in the locations where they operate.

Children

X Freeze is not intended for children. If you believe a child has given us personal information, contact us and we will remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The version shown here is the one that applies, and we will update the date at the top when we make changes.

Contact and data requests

For any privacy question or to ask us to delete your data, email contact@xfreeze.com or use the contact form.

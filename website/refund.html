Refund Policy

How payments, cancellations, and refunds work at X Freeze. Please read this before you buy.

This policy explains when a purchase from X Freeze (xfreeze.com) can be refunded. It applies to everything we sell: Pro subscriptions, bundles, and individual templates. By buying from us you agree to this policy together with our Terms of Service. Last updated July 2026.

Payments for X Freeze are processed by Dodo Payments. Card and billing details are handled by Dodo Payments; we receive order confirmation and plan details only. You can raise a refund request with us using the details below, and we will process it through Dodo Payments when approved.

Everything we sell is digital

X Freeze sells digital goods: template links, motion prompts, and AI skills that you copy and use right away. Access is delivered instantly to your account after payment, so once you have opened, copied, or unlocked an item it is considered used and delivered in full. For that reason, all sales are final and we do not offer refunds for a change of mind, for items you no longer need, or because a third-party tool such as Grok or SuperGrok changed its own pricing, features, or availability.

When we do give a refund

We want you to get what you paid for. We will review and, where fair, refund a purchase in these cases:

  • You were charged but never got access. Payment went through but your Pro plan or purchased item did not unlock, and we cannot fix it for you.
  • Duplicate or accidental charge. You were billed twice for the same thing, or a charge was clearly made in error.
  • A charge you did not authorise. Tell us right away and we will investigate with Dodo Payments.
  • The item is genuinely broken on our side and we are unable to make it work for you within a reasonable time after you report it.

To be eligible, contact us within 7 days of the charge and include your order or payment reference so we can find it quickly.

Pro subscriptions and auto-renewal

Pro Monthly and Pro Yearly are recurring plans. Unless you cancel, they renew automatically at the end of each billing period using your saved payment method, so that your access continues without a break.

  • Cancel anytime. You can cancel from your Account page or by emailing us. Cancellation stops the next renewal.
  • You keep access until the period ends. After you cancel, your Pro features stay active until the end of the period you already paid for. They are not cut off early.
  • No part-period refunds. We do not refund the unused part of a billing period, and we do not give partial refunds for months or days you did not use.
  • Renewal charges. A renewal is not refundable simply because you forgot to cancel. If a renewal charged you within the last few days and you had not used your Pro access in that new period, contact us and we will look at it in good faith.

Bundles and individual templates

Bundles and single template purchases are one-time payments. Once the content is delivered to your account the sale is final, apart from the fault and billing exceptions listed above.

Tips

Tips sent through Buy Me a Coffee are voluntary and are not payments for a product, so they are not refundable.

How to request a refund

Email contact@xfreeze.com or use the contact form with:

  • The email address on your account
  • The order or payment reference from your receipt
  • A short note on what went wrong

We aim to reply within 2 to 3 business days.

How refunds are paid out

Approved refunds go back to your original payment method through Dodo Payments. Once we approve and process a refund it usually reaches you within 5 to 10 business days, though the exact timing depends on your bank or card issuer.

Chargebacks

If something looks wrong with a charge, please contact us first. We can almost always sort it out faster than a bank dispute. Opening a chargeback without contacting us may lead to your account access being paused while the dispute is reviewed.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The version shown here always applies to new purchases, and we will update the date at the top when we make changes.
